# Rank 5 — Study material index

Rank 5 of the 42 common core typically includes the C++ modules **CPP05–CPP09**, major projects **Inception** and **Webserve** or **IRC** (campus branch), and **Exam Rank 05**.

**Branch note:** Many campuses require **Webserve** *or* **IRC** — not both — to advance. Confirm on your intranet.

**Standard:** C++20 at The Hive (`-std=c++20`) for CPP modules; **ft_irc** and **ft_webserve** subject PDFs require **C++98** — see [IRC/exercises.md](./IRC/exercises.md) and [Webserve/exercises.md](./Webserve/exercises.md).
